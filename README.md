# PowerBI
This dashboard is created in Power BI to track the Canadian food service sector performance after pandemic by province and serving channels. Data is sourced from Statistics Canada and is updated every month. 

Key findings: 
- Canadian food service sector kept bouncing back from pandemic since 2021. Total receipts of all four key serving channels across Canada were 100M, 4.4% more than a year ago. 
- The growth was mostly driven by special food service, dine-in restaurant and quick service sector. Drinking places are the only sector with negative change. 
- New Brunswick enjoyed the highest growth due to the contribution from dine-in and quick service restaurants.
